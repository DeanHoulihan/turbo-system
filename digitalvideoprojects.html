Below is a short film project which
I done for my Storytelling and Narrative module.
The film is titled Heart Of The Matter. Password is "CDMStorytelling"

What is the film about?

It is set in a parallel universe, in which Ryker the protagonist is forced to go on a mission to retrieve a human heart for Morken, who is the villain of the story and also a black market organ dealer. Ryker must successfully meet Morken's demands in return to see his girlfriend Destiny ,who has been kidnapped and being held captive by Morken and his gang.
